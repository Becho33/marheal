Origins

Reiki is a fascinating healing art that has its origins in Japan. The word Reiki is made up of two Japanese words: Rei, which means "universal" or "spiritual," and Ki, which means "life force energy."


Reiki is based on the belief that we are all connected to this universal life force energy, and that this energy can be channeled to promote healing and balance in the body. The history of Reiki dates back to the early 20th century, when a Japanese Buddhist monk named Mikao Usui developed the technique.


Usui was on a quest to discover the secrets of healing, and he spent many years studying different healing traditions and spiritual practices. It is said that he had a spiritual awakening after a period of fasting and meditation on a mountain, during which he received the knowledge and power to heal using energy.


Usui went on to teach Reiki to others, and the practice spread throughout Japan and eventually around the world. Over time, different branches of Reiki developed, each with their own unique techniques and philosophies. One of the most interesting aspects of Reiki is its use of symbols and hand positions to channel and direct energy. These symbols are said to have been given to Usui during his spiritual awakening, and are used by Reiki practitioners to enhance the flow of energy and promote healing.


Today, Reiki continues to be practiced by millions of people around the world. It is used to treat a wide range of physical and emotional conditions, and is often used in conjunction with other healing modalities such as massage therapy and acupuncture. Reiki is a powerful tool for promoting health and well-being, and its history and philosophy continue to inspire and fascinate practitioners and scholars alike.


Along the journey

As I embarked on the journey of creating my Reiki organization, I encountered many challenges and lessons along the way. Drawing on my experience in the healing industry, I dedicated myself to the practice of Reiki and its transformative power.


I have faced many trials in my journey, including the steep learning curve of technology and programming. However, my unwavering passion for Reiki and my commitment to helping others has driven me to create a space where people can experience the healing benefits of Reiki in a supportive and welcoming environment.


My journey as a Reiki practitioner has been filled with personal growth and learning. I have worked tirelessly to develop my skills as a practitioner and educator, and I am constantly striving to improve the services I offer to my clients.


I am proud to have created an organization that embodies my values and my commitment to promoting health and well-being through the practice of Reiki. It is my goal to share my knowledge and expertise with others and to help them achieve greater balance and harmony in their lives. I look forward to the opportunity to work with you and support you on your journey towards optimal health and happiness.
